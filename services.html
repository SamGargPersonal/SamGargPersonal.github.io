Tax Advisory & Consulting

Our Services

Comprehensive tax consulting and compliance services across all entity types and industries — delivered by a team with Big 4 pedigree and a solutions-focused mindset.

What We Do

We advise clients across the full spectrum of tax matters — from routine compliance to complex transactions, regulatory submissions, and governance frameworks.

  • Income tax advisory
  • Capital Gains Tax (CGT) advisory
  • International tax advisory
  • Tax advisory and structuring for deals, investments, restructures, transactions and financing
  • Liaison with revenue authorities — rulings, reviews, audits and dispute resolution
  • Submissions and tax advocacy with government authorities and regulators
  • Superannuation tax advisory
  • Tax governance design, testing, reviews and audits
  • Tax training, learning and development support
  • Tax lodgements — returns, activity statements, FIRB tax reports and other filings
  • Preparation / review of tax balance calculations for financial statements
  • Preparation / review of trust distribution statement calculations and disclosures
  • Employee / manager equity and carry interest structuring
  • Employment taxes advisory
  • Goods & Services Tax (GST) advisory
  • Tax information and transparency reporting
  • Tax modelling services
  • Transfer pricing advisory (through strategic arrangements)
  • Stamp duty advisory (through strategic arrangements)

Clients We Serve

We provide the above services to a diverse range of entity types, including listed companies, private companies, small businesses, managed investment trusts, private trusts, discretionary trusts (including family trusts), superannuation funds (large, medium, SMSFs and offshore), family offices / groups, investment houses, sovereign investors, government and public-private partnerships.

Industries: Infrastructure, real estate, private equity, private debt, funds management, superannuation, insurance, banking, financing, services, resources, energy, and government privatisations.
