About OmniTax Professionals

A recently established firm dedicated to providing quality tax services.

OmniTax Professionals is comprised of a network of tax practitioners with significant experience in Big 4 accounting firms who apply a fresh approach to meeting their clients' needs.

Our experience and knowledge across mergers, acquisitions, divestments, refinancings, capital restructures, public offerings, fund and platform establishments, regulator submissions and liaison, tax consolidation, tax modelling, tax distribution/provision calculations, unit pricing, compliance lodgements, tax governance and risk management means we can provide value-added, practical, and solutions-driven service.

OmniTax Professionals is a registered tax agent with the Tax Practitioners Board (TPB) and a registered practice entity member of Chartered Accountants Australia & New Zealand (CAANZ).

Based in Brisbane, Australia, we serve clients throughout Australia and globally.
